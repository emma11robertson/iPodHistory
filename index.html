The History of the iPod

About the iPod

The creation of the iPod changed the electronic world forever. The first iPod was released on October 23, 2001 by Steve Jobs who was one of the founders of Apple alongside Steve Wozniak and Ronald Wayne. There are three types of iPod: shuffle, nano, and touch.
Here are some interesting facts!

  • If you don't use the iPod, the battery can last 14-28 days.
  • It is illegal to play music on full volume in France.
  • The most expensive iPod costs $231,776.
