The History of the iPod

About the iPod

The creation of the iPod changed the electronic world forever. The first iPod was released on October 23, 2001 by Steve Jobs who was one of the founders of Apple alongside Steve Wozniak and Ronald Wayne. There are five types of iPod: classic, mini, shuffle, nano, and touch.
Here are some interesting facts!

  • If you don't use the iPod, the battery can last 14-28 days.
  • It is illegal to play music on an iPod on full volume in France.
  • The most expensive iPod costs $231,776.

Why is the iPod important?

  1. It was an influential design. It has evolved a ton over the years and many other electronic companies use a similar design
  2. It changed the music industry. People are now able to listen to any song they want without needing a radio!

iPod Timeline

October 23, 2001 - The first iPod was released.
November 10, 2001 - The iPod was officially for sale.
January, 2004 - The iPod Mini was released.
January 2005 - The iPod Shuffle was released.
